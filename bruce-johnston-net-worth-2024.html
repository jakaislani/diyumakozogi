    Early Life and Career Beginnings

    In 1965, Johnston joined the Beach Boys, replacing Glen Campbell. He contributed significantly to the band’s success, both as a vocalist and a songwriter. His most notable work with the band includes the album “Pet Sounds” and the hit single “Good Vibrations”.
