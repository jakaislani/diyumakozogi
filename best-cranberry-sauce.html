    Best Cranberry Sauce (Cranberry Relish, Too!)

    The best cranberry sauce is sweet, tangy, and adds some variety to your holiday plate, offsetting the saltiness of all those savory sides. But which is the best canned cranberry sauce you can buy? We broke out the can opener to find out. 

    Jellied or whole berry—that is the question. Personally, I’m a jellied cranberry sauce gal. There’s nothing like a fat slab of can-shaped tangy goo to awaken the palate when you’re knee deep in turkey, gravy, and a dozen different beige casseroles. You might have assumed that most canned cranberry sauce—whether it’s jellied cranberry sauce, whole berry cranberry sauce, or even cranberry relish—is basically the same. But over the course of two separate taste tests, we discovered that there’s a huge variety in flavor and texture. And, I have to say, whole berry cranberry sauce may not be my thing, but I tasted a few that were good enough to convert me. Whether you’re cool with skins and seeds or you want the iconic slimy sameness of jellied sauce, this is the best cranberry sauce you can buy.

    Best Sweet

    Great Value Jellied Cranberry SauceWalmart

    This cranberry sauce from Walmart lacks some of the punchy tartness we loved in the higher-ranking sauces on this list, but it still tastes really good. If you want a cranberry sauce that’s basically like a dinnertime dessert, this is the best cranberry sauce out there. It’s super sweet, but not nauseatingly sweet, and it still packs plenty of cranberry flavor (although not quite as much as Ocean Spray). As store brands go, this is a great jellied cranberry sauce.

    7.5/10

    Best Texture

    Sprouts Organic Whole Berry Cranberry SauceInstacart

    If the slimy nature of jellied cranberry sauce is too freaky for you, give this whole berry sauce from Sprouts a shot. Some of the whole berry sauces we tried were positively packed with seeds. Yes, the seeds add texture, but they also wind up getting stuck in your teeth and staying there until Christmas. This sauce has chunks, but isn’t overly seedy. Basically, it offers texture without any unpleasantness, and it tastes good, too—tart and fresh and festive. I also think this is the best canned cranberry sauce if you want something that will look nice on a holiday table. I’ll be the first to admit that can-shaped cranberry sauce isn’t the classiest thing to have on the table when you’re having company. 

    Credit: Sarah Demonteverde / Instacart

    Rating:

    8/10

    Best for a Sandwich

    Stater Bros. Jellied Cranberry SauceInstacart

    The best thing about Thanksgiving dinner is the leftovers. Stuffing you can eat with your fingers out of a Tupperware container throughout the day. Casseroles that make the next week and a half feel like a holiday every day. And, of course, leftover turkey for sandwiches. This bright and tangy jellied sauce from Stater Bros. is the best cranberry sauce to slice up and stack on some sourdough bread with thick slabs of turkey breast, stuffing, and gravy. It’s an excellent argument for buying at least a couple extra cans of cranberry sauce when you do your Thanksgiving shopping this year. Especially if you’re doing your shopping at Stater Bros.

    Best Cranberry Relish

    Harry & David Country Cranberry RelishHarry & David

    This cranberry relish from Harry & David scored high marks in our second taste test because it’s just so interesting. Prior to this taste test, I wasn’t totally certain what the difference is between whole berry cranberry sauce and cranberry relish. And, honestly, after tasting a couple of other brands of cranberry relish, I wasn’t any closer to understanding. That’s what makes Harry & David the best cranberry relish—it’s definitely different from cranberry sauce. It’s very much its own thing. It’s made with walnuts, and the nuts give it excellent texture and a nutty flavor that really works well with the lovely halved cranberries and orange zest in the mix. It’s very sweet—if you want straight-up tartness from cranberry sauce, there are better options on this list. But this is unique and it’s going to be absolutely outstanding on your leftovers sandwiches. It’s a little pricier than the canned cranberry sauce on this list, but it doubles as a really nice gift for whomever is hosting Thanksgiving this year. 

    Credit: Liv Averett / Harry & David

    Best Jellied

    Ocean Spray Jellied Cranberry SauceWalmart

    Ocean Spray is the king of cranberries and, perhaps predictably, the brand’s jellied cranberry sauce is the best jellied cranberry sauce we tried: tart, full of bold cranberry flavor, and devoid of the unpleasant tinny flavor we detected in some of the canned cranberry sauces we tried. Having tried Ocean Spray’s jellied cranberry sauce alongside multiple store brands, I have to say, this is a situation in which buying the name brand is the way to go. It just has so, so much more cranberry flavor. “This feels like it has depth,” one taste tester remarked. Sadly, the same can’t be said for Ocean Spray’s whole berry sauce. The cranberry flavor was just so much more concentrated in the jellied variety—the brand’s whole berry didn’t even make it onto our list! If jellied cranberry sauce is your jam, this is by far the best cranberry sauce for your Thanksgiving spread.

    Credit: Sarah Demonteverde / Walmart

    Best of the Best

    Stater Bros. Whole Cranberry SauceInstacart

    Yes, my loyalties lie with jellied cranberry sauce—the kind with no visible relationship to cranberries whatsoever—but this whole cranberry sauce is like a wonderful hybrid of jellied and homemade. Intermingled in its sweet jellied base, Stater Bros. Whole Cranberry Sauce has big pieces of berry that burst in your mouth and it doesn’t suffer from being absolutely packed with skins and seeds like some of the other whole berry cranberry sauces we tried. If you have a Stater Bros. in your area, this is the best cranberry sauce you can buy.
