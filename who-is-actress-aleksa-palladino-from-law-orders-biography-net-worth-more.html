    Who is Actress Aleksa Palladino from “Law & Order”s: Biography, Net Worth & More- Aleksa Palladino, an American aged 42 is a famous actress widely known for her role as Chris Lasorda and Lo in the respective films Before the Devil Knows You’re Dead (2007) and Manny & Lo (1996). She is also notable for her recurring role as Angela Darmody on the HBO series Boardwalk Empire.

    Who is Aleksa Palladino?

    On September 21, 1980, Aleksa Palladino was born in New York City in the United States of America.

    What is Aleksa Palladino’s profession?

    Her debut role was as Lo in the critically-acclaimed Manny & Lo opposite Scarlett Johansson, where she played a 16-year-old character despite being 14 at the time. The next year she was given her first lead role in the short film Number One Fan with Glenn Fitzgerald, and soon appeared in Wrestling with Alligators with Joely Richardson, the well-received The Adventures of Sebastian Cole with Adrien Grenier, and Second Skin with Fitzgerald again.

    The year 2000 saw the release of Palladino’s Red Dirt, followed by the independent film Lonesome, and Storytelling with Selma Blair. She then had guest appearances in Law & Order: Criminal Intent, The Sopranos (where she played 2 different characters), and Law & Order. In 2003, Palladino moved to Los Angeles, married Devon Church at a scenic overlook on Mulholland Drive, and began writing music with her husband as the band Exitmusic. Exitmusic released a debut album in the Fall of 2007.

    Their follow-up is due in early 2010 on the Manimal Vinyl label. In 2004, Aleksa returned to acting with a guest appearance on Medium, followed by her lead role in Spectropia and a supporting one in Find Me Guilty with Vin Diesel. After working in Find Me Guilty, the director Sidney Lumet offered Palladino the role of Chris Lasorda in Before the Devil Knows You’re Dead, which she kindly accepted. She then filmed The Picture of Dorian Gray, based on the novel by Oscar Wilde, and landed a supporting lead role in the well-acclaimed Horror sequel Wrong Turn 2: Dead End.

    Who is Aleksa Palladino married to?

    The actress is divorced. She was once married to Devon Church. The duo tied the knot in 2003. Unfortunately, they divorce in 2018 and went their separate ways.
