    Boosie Badazz discovered new alleged paperwork on Terrance “Gangsta” Williams. This morning, Boosie took to Instagram where he shared a photo of the paperwork that reportedly states Williams helped police solve over 40 homicides. The alleged paperwork states, “The New Orleans Cold Case Homicide division sent a letter to the USAD of the Eastern District Of Lousiana (“EDLA”) in which it outlined Williams’ cooperation in 40 cold case homicides.” It continues, “In an attempt to reduce his life sentence, Williams closed 40 cold case homicides.”

    However, if you look a bit deeper into the paperwork, it reads that Williams admitted to several of the murders. Still, Boosie had a field day with the information. “SO HE TOLD ON 40 DEAD PEOPLE,” Boosie wrote. “HELL NA HE TOLD ON THE WHOLE NEW ORLEANS WTF he look like #mastersplinter on this pic lol SAMMY THE BULL HAS BEEN OUT DONE BY A N***A FROM LOUISIANA,” he added. “THIS AINT BLACK HISTORY THIS RAT HISTORY WTF EVERYBODY SHOULD RUN WHEN U SEE THIS MAN," he added.

    Boosie Badazz’s Beef With Terrance “Gangsta” Williams

    Issued flared up between Boosie Badazz & Terrence “Gangsta” Williams last month after the “Wipe Me Down” rapper’s interview with Vlad TV. As Boosie denounced T.I. for supposedly telling on his dead cousin to get out of a gun charge, Williams questioned Badazz’s stance on the matter. “You also admitted you don’t mess with rats at all,” he said in a video shared to his YouTube page. “You and him have a good relationship, so have you cut T.I. off period? Or was that just a stunt on Vlad for the people to see that y’all still talk?"

    Afterward, Terrance Williams shared a video of Boosie claiming to have filed a police report against the police, as well as a civil suit, over stolen jewelry. Afterward, Boosie went off on Williams in a series of homophobic tweets. “NEW ORLEANS SHOULD BAN THIS DUDE ‼️THIS IS THE WORST KINDA N***A‼️ HE A RAT , A FAMILY SNAKE , N A HOMOSEXUAL PLAYIN GANGSTER,” Boosie wrote. So far, Williams hasn’t responded to the paperwork but we’ll keep you posted if he does.
