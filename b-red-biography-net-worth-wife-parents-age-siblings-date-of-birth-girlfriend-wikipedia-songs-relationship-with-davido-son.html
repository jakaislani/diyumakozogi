    Biography

    B-Red (born July 23), whose real name is addressed as Adebayo Adeleke, is a talented Nigerian singer, songwriter and businessman.

    He is the son of a famous politician in Nigeria who hails from Osun State. His father allegedly won the 2019 gubernatorial election held in Osun state but was denied his term after the scandal over his WAEC certificate.

    B-Red is also known for being the cousin of Nigerian pop star Davido. He also has an older brother named Sina Rambo, also signed to the HKN Music label. B-Red is a versatile singer with Afro Pop and R&B music genres.

    Early Life & Education

    Born in Atlanta, Georgia, the United States, the son of Nigerian senator Ademola Adeleke, B-Red relocated to Nigeria to pursue music as a career.

    His father’s name is Ademola Adeleke, while his mother is Christina Adeleke.

    B-Red is a cousin of the Nigerian singer Davido. His brother’s name is Sina Rambo. He was born in Atlanta, Georgia, United States, on July 23. 

    He is the son of a famous senator from the Federal Republic of Nigeria, Senator Ademola Adeleke, also known for his dance moves.

    Career

    B-Red is currently signed to the HKN Music record label alongside his older brother, Sina Rambo.

    Officially, the dancehall artiste, B-Red, began his musical career in February 2013 with his RnB singe “Insane Girl,” widely accepted by music lovers in the Nigerian music industry.

    The single “Insane Girl” featured Davido and was produced by Shizzi. Before releasing his hit single, B-Red toured the world with his then label mate and cousin, Davido working and gaining an audience as his hype man.

    In September 2015, B-Red accompanied Davido on his UK tour as a supporting artiste and made his international debut. B-Red featured Akon, his first international single, “Cucumber.”

    B-Red has worked with top musical acts in the Nigerian entertainment industry. He has collaborated with Davido, Don jazzy, Tiwa Savage, Runtown, CDQ, and Lil Kesh.

    He achieved tremendous success in the Nigerian entertainment industry, with some of his singles becoming one of radio and televisions favourite. The video to his single “Twerk” hit over one million views, which increased B-Red net worth.

    According to him, B-Red is a combination of “Bayo and Red,” where B represents Bayo, and Red represents fair skin due to complexion. Red is a general term used to describe fair-skinned people in the United States.

    The Afropop singer is known for his attractive appearance, making him the “ladies’ man.”

    Personal Life

    Senator Adeleke’s youngest son, B-Red, announced the purchase of his 120 million naira mansion in Lagos via his Instagram page, which means that B-Red net worth has also increased.

    Singer B-Red is a married man, he is legally married to Faith Johnson. Their union is blessed with a son named Jordan Adeleke.
