Peter worked with him again to write a couple of songs for the soundtrack of the famous film “The Scarlet Letter” (1995). He has toured the world in support of his performance “Life Is What You Make It: A Concert & Conversation with Peter Buffett.” His book, “Life Is What You Make It: Find Your Path to Fulfillment,” was released in May 2010 and was praised by several famous people, including Ted Turner, former President Bill Clinton, and Bill Gates.
