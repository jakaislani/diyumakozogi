    Matt Hughes Injury And Accident: What Happened ToThe Storm Chaser?

    When Matt’s death was revealed after a Storm Chasers episode in November 2010, his fans were devastated.

    The fact that he had eventually passed suddenly from complications after a suicide attempt over six months earlier, on May 26, 2010, was even more shocking.

    He supposedly tried to commit suicide on May 14 but was discovered alive. First responders urgently assessed his condition.

    In a blog post about his days in intensive care, Aaron Blaser, a close friend and colleague of Matt’s, stated, “There was a 24-hour vigil outside the waiting room, family, other chasers, business friends.”

    Aaron continued, “I knew we were going to lose him. ” Matt Hughes’ suicide attempt was influenced by the fact that he struggled with depression and nobody were aware of it.

    Matt died from the injuries he acquired after hanging himself after spending less than ten days in intensive care.

    Matt Hughes Death: Tributes For The Brave Storm Chaser

    Matt received numerous heartfelt condolences from Discovery producers and friends, and family.

    Matt was honored in the Storm Chasers episode on November 3, 2010. His final storm engagement was among them.

    His family released a statement a few days after the news of his death was made public.

    The statement stated that “Matt Hughes was genuinely a wonderful and caring person with a warm and loving soul, in the perception of his various relatives, friends, and coworkers.

    The Hughes family went on to state that their intention was never to hide the truth about Matt’s passing to mislead the public.

    But instead, to give family space to grieve privately and safeguard the several young children this tragedy has impacted.

    They also highlighted the devastating effects of depression and noted that it is a treatable condition despite its huge incidence.
